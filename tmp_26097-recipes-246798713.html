Taco Salad

Ingredients:

Ground Beef 1 lb.
Shredded Lettuce 1 bag (2 cups)
Shredded Cheese (Taco Blend) 1 bag (2 cups)
Diced Tomatoes 1 can
Diced Olives 1 can
Refried Beans 1 can
Taco Seasoning 1 packet
Catalina Dressing (12oz. Classic Style) 1/2 bottle
Nacho Cheese Doritos 1 bag

Directions:

  1. Cook ground beef in skillet over medium heat until
    browned and crumbly, about 10 minutes. Drain excess
    grease and stir in taco seasoning. Remove from heat
    and let stand until cool.
  2. drain tomatoes and olives thoroughly.
  3. mix all ingredients.
  4. serve immediately and enjoy!

Frito Chili Pie

Ingredients:

Fritos chips 1 bag
Chili 1 can
Shredded Cheese 1 bag
Diced Tomatoes (optional) 1 can
Diced Onion (optional) 1/4 slice

Directions:

  1. cook chili and cheese until cheese melts
  2. mix ingredients
  3. serve immediately and enjoy!

Bacon Cheddar Deviled Eggs

Ingredients:

Eggs 12
Mayonnaise 1/2 cup
Bacon 4 slices
Finely Shredded Cheese 2 tbsp
Mustard 1 tbsp

Directions:

  1. Place eggs in a saucepan, and cover with cold water.
    Bring water to a boil and immediately remove from heat.
    Cover, and let eggs stand in hot water for 10 to 12 minutes.
    Remove from hot water, and cool.
  2. Meanwhile, place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over
    medium-high heat until evenly brown. Alternatively, wrap bacon
    in paper towels and cook in the microwave for about 1 minute
    per slice. Crumble and set aside.
  3. Peel the eggs, and cut in half lengthwise. Remove yolks to a
    small bowl. Mash egg yolks with mayonnaise, crumbled bacon
    and cheese. Stir in mustard. Fill egg white halves with the
    yolk mixture and refrigerate until serving.
